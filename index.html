EHCHOES OF THE PAST.

Madam Darura’s journey began from humble beginnings, with nothing but a dream to uplift children from the hardships of their circumstances. Starting from zero, she faced countless struggles—poverty, societal skepticism, and the weight of a system that often overlooked the most vulnerable. But with unwavering determination and a heart full of compassion, she established Ajir Happy Kids School. It wasn’t easy—she faced financial challenges, a lack of resources, and skepticism from those who doubted a woman could lead such a change. Yet, with each obstacle, she grew stronger, and the smiles of her students fueled her fight. Madam Darura’s vision wasn’t just to educate, but to transform lives—turning “zero” into “hero,” one child at a time. Through sacrifice, perseverance, and a heart full of hope, she made her dream a reality, leaving a legacy of hope for generations to come.

HISTORY OF THE GREAT SCHOOL AJIR.

Ajir Secondary School, located in Wajir County, has earned its reputation as one of the best educational institutions in the region since its establishment in 2004. With a commitment to excellence, the school has consistently delivered quality education, producing top-performing students in both academics and extracurricular activities. Over the years, Ajir has become a symbol of resilience and ambition, overcoming challenges in an arid region to provide a nurturing environment for young minds. Its dedicated staff, modern facilities, and strong community support have played a crucial role in shaping future leaders of the county and beyond.
